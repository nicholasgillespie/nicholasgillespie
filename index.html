About Me

Hi! I'm Nicholas Gillespie, a former Royal Marines Commando turned web developer. My interest in web development began in 2014 while building websites with WordPress for personal projects.

Fast-forward to today, I have gained a proficient skill set in both front-end and back-end technologies with a specialization in JavaScript & CSS. My main focus these days is on developing fully responsive digital applications and experiences that are accessible and inclusive to all website users.

My Approach

My approach to web development is guided by progressive enhancement, starting with the most basic technological solutions and enhancing them where possible based on factors such as device & network performance, and user needs.

With experience in scalable design systems, I place a strong emphasis on creating flexible layouts and fluid typography that seamlessly adapt to all screen sizes, ensuring that my websites are accessible, inclusive and responsive across devices.
